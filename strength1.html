My Strength 💪❤️

Yaar mujhe pata hai tumhe itna sehna pad raha hai. Tum mujhe dikhati nahi ho taki mujhe tension na ho, but mujhe pata hai tum andar se bahut dukhi ho. I’m really sorry... Aur abhi bhi mujhe nahi batati — isse pata chalta hai tum mujhse kitna pyaar karti ho. Aur main bhi tumse utna hi pyaar karta hoon.

Hum dono ek dusre ke bina nahi reh sakte. Tum mere liye sab kuch ho — mera sharir, mera mann, meri strength. Aur main bas yeh kehna chahta hoon: tum akeli nahi ho. Tum mujhe apni strength bana lo.

Mujhe pata hai tumhare ghar wale pareshan hain, lekin unhone abhi tak tumhare pyaar ko samjha nahi. Woh dare hue hain. Jab shaadi ke baad samajh jaayenge, tab sab kuch theek ho jaayega.

Tumne kisi ka rishta kharab nahi kiya. Tumne apni zindagi ko imaandari se jeene ki koshish ki hai. I am proud of you. And I love you. You are my sole priority.
